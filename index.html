Shorthand & Keyboard Practice Guidelines
Shorthand Transcription Routine:

Listen to 120 WPM dictation for 5 minutes. Transcribe immediately onto PC. Count your omissions and spellings. Aim for less than 1.5% outline errors.

Keyboard Touch Typing Rules:

Never look at your hands! If you get stuck, look at the virtual keyboard display above. Rest index fingers on F and J (homing bumps) and use correct fingers for other keys.

Aiwan-e-Sadr Exam Strategy:

Secretariat speed tests are strict. Practice daily row drills to push muscle memory so typing words becomes an automatic reflex during shorthand translation.

Secretariat Correspondence Formats

General Formatting Guidelines:

  • Font: Times New Roman, Size 12. Line spacing is 1.5.
  • Margins: Left margin 1.5 inches (for binding/file holes), top/bottom/right 1 inch.
  • Official Titles: The name of the government department, file references, dates, and names of officers should be capitalized exactly as shown.
  • Sign-off: The signature is followed by the officer's name in brackets, designation, and telephone number.
